NaughtyDog

About

Founded in 1984, Naughty Dog is one of the most successful and prolific game development studios in the world and one of PlayStation Studios' flagship studios. From the creation of the iconic Crash Bandicoot and Jak and Daxter series to modern franchises like Uncharted and The Last of Us, Naughty Dog is responsible for some of the most critically acclaimed and commercially successful games on Sony's PlayStation platforms.

Through the use of cutting-edge technology and evocative, character-driven storytelling, Naughty Dog has received hundreds of industry and media awards, while developing a passionate fan base of millions of players worldwide.

Recent Naughty Dog releases include 2016's Uncharted 4: A Thief's End, winner of multiple Game of the Year awards, and 2017's Uncharted: The Lost Legacy - a new standalone adventure in the Uncharted series. As of December 2017, total global sales for the Uncharted franchise have surpassed 41.7 million copies sold.

Naughty Dog's latest title is The Last of Us Part II, the long-awaited sequel to 2013's The Last of Us, which is available now for PlayStation 4.
