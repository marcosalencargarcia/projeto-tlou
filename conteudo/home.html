What is the history of the franchise about? - NaughtyDog

The Last of Us is an action-adventure survival video game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released exclusively for PlayStation 3 on June 14, 2013. In the story, players control Joel, a man tasked with escorting a teenage girl named Ellie across a post-apocalyptic United States. The Last of Us is played from a third-person perspective, with players using firearms, improvised weapons, and stealth in order to defend against hostile humans and cannibalistic creatures infected by a mutated Cordyceps fungus. A "Listening Mode" allows the location of enemies through enhanced listening and spatial awareness.
